Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 22, 2025

Backyard Par values your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information, in compliance with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and applicable laws.

Information We Collect

  • Personal: Name, email, phone number, event details, billing address, and event city.
  • Payment: Processed via third-party providers; we do not store credit card numbers.
  • Technical: Browser, IP address, device info, and site usage (cookies/analytics).
  • Communications: Emails, notes, and inquiries you send us.

How We Use Information

  • Manage bookings, process payments, and send invoices/receipts.
  • Communicate about reservations and respond to inquiries.
  • Send occasional promotions or updates (opt-out anytime).
  • Improve services and the website.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers (payment, booking, email) and legal authorities if required.

Your Rights (CCPA)

California residents may request to know what we collect, request deletion, opt out of data sale (we do not sell data), and will not be discriminated against for exercising rights. Contact bookings@backyardpar.com.

Data Retention

We retain personal information as long as necessary to fulfill bookings, comply with laws, and resolve disputes. You may request deletion at any time.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards (SSL, secure storage, limited access) to protect your information.

Cookies & Tracking

Our site may use cookies/analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser; some features may not function properly.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us to request deletion.

Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Updates will be posted with a new “Last updated” date.

Questions? bookings@backyardpar.com
