About Backyard Par

We’re on a mission to bring golf’s joy straight to your event.

Our Story

Backyard Par started with a simple idea: golf is more fun when everyone can play—anywhere. We built a premium mobile simulator experience that fits in driveways, backyards, and event spaces, complete with a pro-grade launch monitor, giant screen, and an on-site tech to run the show.

Whether it’s a birthday, team offsite, or fundraiser, we bring the course to you so your guests can swing, compete, and laugh together.

What We Value

Fun First

We design for smiles and hype. The simulator is a party magnet.

Professionalism

On-time, tidy setup, and reliable equipment at every event.

Inclusive Play

Righty/lefty clubs and game modes for every skill level.
