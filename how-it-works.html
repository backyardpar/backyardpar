How It Works

Effortless setup. Big-time fun. Here’s what to expect.

Your Event in 3 Simple Steps

1

Book & Confirm

Choose your preferred date and package. We’ll confirm all the details and requirements with you within 24 hours to secure your event.

2

We Arrive & Set Up

On the day of your event, our professional team will arrive, set up the inflatable enclosure, projector, and state-of-the-art launch monitor in just 60–90 minutes.

3

Enjoy & Play

Your guests can enjoy a variety of golf courses and game modes. We provide both right- and left-handed clubs, and our technician is on hand to assist with the fun.

Frequently Asked Questions
