How It Works

Effortless setup. Big-time fun. Here’s what to expect.

1) We Arrive & Set Up

We bring the inflatable enclosure, projector, launch monitor, and everything needed.

2) Game Time

Choose from multiple game modes and challenges. Clubs provided (or bring your own).

3) Pack Up

We tear down quickly and leave your space as we found it.

Event Requirements

  • • Outdoor setup only. Flat, open area (min 15’ W × 20’ D × 10’ H).
  • • One standard power outlet within 75 feet.
  • • Setup time ~45–60 minutes. We handle everything.
